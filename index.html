How does it work?

Our intent is to provide a physical object that will be your reminder in real life to not forget to practice.

It works based on a simple set of rules.

I haven't practiced yet today
The lamp will take a different color each hour that passes.
I've already practiced today
The lamp acts as a regular lamp.
I've broken my streak
The lamp takes a pink color until you start a new streak.
